Length

Length is a measurement of distance. It can be measured in units such as meters, centimeters, or kilometers.

  • 1 kilometer = 1000 meters
  • 1 meter = 100 centimeters
  • 1 foot = 12 inches

Area

Area is the measure of how much space a flat surface covers. Common units of area include square meters and square kilometers.

  • Area of a rectangle = length × width
  • Area of a circle = π × radius²

Volume

Volume is the amount of space an object takes up. It is measured in cubic units such as cubic meters or liters.

  • Volume of a cube = side³
  • Volume of a cylinder = π × radius² × height

Time

Time is a measure of the ongoing sequence of events, measured in seconds, minutes, hours, etc.

  • 1 hour = 60 minutes
  • 1 minute = 60 seconds
  • 1 day = 24 hours
